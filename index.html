Instructions:

Beef Stroganoff

Tender beef strips in a rich, creamy mushroom sauce served over egg noodles. A classic comfort food dish.

‚è±Ô∏è 45 mins üë• 6 servings ‚≠ê 4.9/5

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef sirloin, sliced thin
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 12 oz egg noodles

Instructions:

  1. Cook egg noodles according to package directions
  2. Brown beef strips in large skillet
  3. Add mushrooms and onions, cook until soft
  4. Sprinkle with flour and add broth gradually
  5. Simmer until thickened, stir in sour cream
  6. Serve over noodles
